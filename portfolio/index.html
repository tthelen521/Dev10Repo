About Me

Hello, my name is Tanner Thelen. I attended college at Iowa State University where I received a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science. I chose to study Computer Science because it gave me the opportunity to solve complex problems while learning how to create software. I believe that it is becoming more evident every day that software development and information technology as a whole are becoming integral parts of nearly every industry. I am excited to be a part of that monumental shift in society.

While studying at Iowa State, I learned not only of the theoretical aspects of Computer Science, but of some of the various programming languages and development practices vital to software engineering. I've mainly used Java and C for most of my programming projects and have used C++ on occasions as well. I also have exposure through coursework to web development, database design and software testing(TDD). I have experience in using both Windows and Linux and have worked on projects in both operating systems.
